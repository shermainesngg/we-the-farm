## Design Context

### Users
Community members in Singapore — volunteers, neighbours, families, students — discovering or joining a rooftop farm. Browsed casually on phones and desktops, often through Instagram links.

### Brand Personality
Rooted, communal, sun-warmed. A passion project, not a corporate venture. Feels like a hand-printed farmers' market poster on thick recycled stock.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial farm-to-table, inspired by Homegrown sustainable sandwiches. Full-bleed photography hero, asymmetric editorial grids with alternating image/text blocks, generous whitespace, warm earthy tones on cream backgrounds. Transparent overlay nav. Light theme (daytime outdoor community context).

### Typography
- **Headings**: Vollkorn — warm organic serif with craftsman quality, variable 400–900
- **Body**: Albert Sans — clean warm grotesque, readable, variable 100–900

### Color Palette
- Forest: #2D5016 (primary green, hero backgrounds)
- Forest Light: #4A7C34
- Sage: #7BA05B (secondary green, icons)
- Cream: #FDF8F0 (primary background)
- Cream Dark: #F0E8D8 (secondary surface)
- Terracotta: #C2703E (CTA accent, links)
- Earth: #4A3628 (headings, dark surfaces)
- Amber: #D4A853 (highlight accent, labels)
- Soil: #3E2723 (body text)

### Design Principles
1. **Photography-first** — Large images tell the story; text supports, not dominates
2. **Editorial asymmetry** — Alternating left-right content blocks, not uniform grids
3. **Generous breathing room** — Whitespace is a feature, not wasted space
4. **Organic warmth** — Every element should feel handmade and intentional, not templated
5. **Quiet hierarchy** — Small-caps labels, serif headings, muted body text create layers without shouting
